Readings Table

Table containing readings.

  • run:which run the readings are in. Must be in run_info.
  • t:timestamp
  • raw_ch:chainage of the reading
  • rl:reading
  • s_point:start point
  • e_point:end point
  • vect:linestring geometry from start_point to end_point
  • ps_text:possible sections and directions that the reading could be in
  • ps:sec_rev possible sections and directions that the reading could be in
