Processing

Data is processed in the following stages:

Fitting

The routes table is used to fit readings to a section,direction,xsp and section chainage;creating the fitted table.

Resizing

The resized_table table is created from 100m segments of every section,direction and xsp in the requested table.

The RL in the resized table is calculated as the weighted(by overlap) mean rl of all readings overlapping the 100m range.
